## Why I Started Writing

Writing helps me think. When I explain something in plain language, I usually discover I didn't understand it as well as I thought. So this blog is partly selfish — it forces me to close that gap.

I'll be writing about things I'm actually working on: software architecture, TypeScript patterns, frontend performance, and the occasional lesson learned the hard way.

## What to Expect

- **Technical deep-dives** — not "here's the API," but "here's why this tradeoff exists"
- **Short notes** — quick observations from day-to-day work
- **Project breakdowns** — what I built, what I'd do differently

## A Note on the Stack

This blog runs on [Next.js](https://nextjs.org) with MDX for content and [Giscus](https://giscus.app) for comments (backed by GitHub Discussions). Posts are plain `.mdx` files in the repo — no CMS, no database round-trips.

If you have thoughts on anything I write, the comment section below is open.
